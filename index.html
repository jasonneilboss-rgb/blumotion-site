Welcome

Premier transfers, personally handled.

BluMotion was formed to provide exclusive, reliable and affordable shuttles to corporate clients and government delegations. We pride ourselves on a reputation for personalised, premier-quality service.

At BluMotion Executive Shuttle, only the best will do. Our trained, professional personnel make sure you travel safely, comfortably and in style.

We are a 100% black-owned company, contributing to the national effort to reduce poverty by empowering previously disadvantaged people.

What we do

Services

From airport runs to inter-province journeys — booked once, handled completely.

Airport Transfers

No expensive parking, and no car left exposed at the terminal. We collect you from your door to the airport, and from the airport to your home, office or hotel — in air-conditioned comfort.

Point to Point

Personalised, convenient transfers without endless separate bookings. Your driver waits patiently while you attend to your business, then takes you on.

City to City

Travel safely and comfortably between major cities and destinations across the provinces. We move large groups of travellers, too.
